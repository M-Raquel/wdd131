Overview

Purpose

A personal porfolio/blog to showcase the various projects, interests, and thoughts that make up who I am as a person.

Audience

The audience would include future employeers who are looking to see what my skills are for a company/project. It would also be for myself to help keep track of personal progress.

Dynamic elements

Using object array's to bring in images and use template literals. A way to change from a light to dark mode by hiding classes with javascript. Also add filters so people can search between topics/connections.
